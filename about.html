About St. Mark's Academy

Established to serve families in and around Roorkee, St. Mark's Academy blends academic rigour with moral education. Our approach is simple: clear expectations, supportive teaching, and steady practice. Students participate in assemblies, house activities, sports meets, debates, and service projects that build confidence and character. We do not offer hostel facilities or specialised labs.

Vision

To shape thoughtful, resilient and compassionate young people.

Mission

Deliver engaging learning experiences and uphold values of honesty, humanity and humility.

Outcomes

Strong fundamentals, clear communication, teamwork, and responsible citizenship.
